About GL Generational

A proudly South African-founded B2B supply company built on service and reliability.

Our Story

Built on the belief that businesses deserve better supply.

GL Generational Project Pty Ltd was founded with a singular mission: make it easy for organisations of all sizes to source quality workplace supplies from a single, trustworthy partner.

We saw businesses spending too much time juggling multiple vendors, chasing delayed deliveries, and overpaying for common necessities. We set out to fix that — offering a comprehensive catalogue across four key categories with unmatched service levels and competitive pricing.

Today we proudly serve small businesses, large corporates, government departments, schools, hospitals, and construction firms — adapting to each client's unique needs while maintaining our founding commitment to service excellence.
